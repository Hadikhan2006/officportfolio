IT Company Website

Corporate Website Design

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript Bootstrap

Project Overview

The IT Company Website is a comprehensive corporate website designed to showcase the services, portfolio, and team of a technology company. The project focuses on creating a professional and engaging online presence that effectively communicates the company's expertise and capabilities to potential clients.

The design features a modern, clean layout with interactive elements that enhance user engagement. With sections dedicated to services, portfolio, team members, and a careers page, the website serves as a complete digital representation of the company's brand and offerings.

Key Features

Comprehensive Service Showcase

Detailed presentation of the company's core services including web development, mobile app development, and digital marketing with visually appealing icons and descriptions.

Interactive Statistics Counter

Animated counters that highlight the company's achievements, including working hours, completed projects, happy clients, and awards received.

Team Member Profiles

Professional presentation of team members with photos, roles, and social media links to build trust and showcase the expertise behind the company.

Integrated Careers Section

Dedicated careers page with a job application form that allows potential candidates to submit their information and resume directly through the website.

Interactive FAQ Section

Accordion-style FAQ section that provides answers to common questions about the company's services, pricing, and processes in an easily digestible format.
